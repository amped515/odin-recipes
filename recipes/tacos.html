Easy Beef Tacos

Back to Home

Description

These easy beef tacos are quick to make and perfect for a weeknight dinner. The seasoned ground beef is packed into warm tortillas and topped with fresh lettuce, cheese, salsa, and sour cream for a simple meal that is always a hit.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 8 small flour or corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 diced tomato
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Optional: sliced jalapeños or avocado

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the ground beef until browned, breaking it apart as it cooks.
  2. Drain the excess grease, then stir in the taco seasoning and water.
  3. Simmer for 5 minutes until the meat is evenly coated and the sauce thickens slightly.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet, microwave, or oven.
  5. Fill each tortilla with the seasoned beef.
  6. Top with lettuce, cheese, tomato, salsa, sour cream, and any other toppings you like.
  7. Serve immediately while warm.
