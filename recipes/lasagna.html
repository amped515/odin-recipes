Classic Beef Lasagna

Back to Home

Description

This classic beef lasagna is a hearty, comforting dinner made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella. It takes a little time to put together, but the end result is worth it and makes great leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package directions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef and onion over medium heat until the beef is browned. Drain excess grease.
  4. Add the garlic, marinara sauce, tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese and egg until combined.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, then ricotta mixture, then meat sauce, then mozzarella. Repeat the layers.
  7. Finish with sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan on top.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake another 15 minutes until bubbly and lightly browned.
  9. Let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
