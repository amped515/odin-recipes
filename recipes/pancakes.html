Fluffy Buttermilk Pancakes

Back to Home

Description

These fluffy buttermilk pancakes are soft, light, and perfect for breakfast. They cook up golden brown and pair well with butter, maple syrup, fresh berries, or chocolate chips if you want to dress them up a little.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Butter or oil for the pan

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix; a few lumps are fine.
  4. Heat a lightly buttered skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake onto the skillet.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with butter and maple syrup.
